Dressing Stars Since 2013...

Hi! I am Norma, the founder of Stardust Lab and a Fashion and Jewelry designer based in Copenhagen. I started sewing my bikinis back 2013 when I won my class in Bikini Fitness. Since then I became passionate about improving my physique and presentation. And so I did until reaching top 6 at European Championships.
My mission is to create the winner look that works for you. I know all the tricks and rules on how to make the best out of your physique. I can help choosing the colors, the cut and the design to maximize your chances of success, and I am aware of all international trends and classes.
Trust my experience of 10 years dressing stars!
